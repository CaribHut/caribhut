# Carib Hut Restaurant Website - PRD

## Original Problem Statement
Build a website for Caribbean restaurant "Carib Hut" in Västerås, Sweden with menu tab, about us tab, and drinks tab.

## User Personas
- Local Västerås residents seeking Caribbean cuisine
- Food enthusiasts looking for authentic Caribbean flavors in Sweden
- Groups celebrating events wanting to book tables

## Core Requirements
- Navigation: Home, Menu, Drinks, About Us, Contact sections
- Menu: Display food items with prices, descriptions, and tags (spicy, vegan, popular, new)
- Drinks: Cocktails, mocktails, rum drinks, beer, wine categories
- About Us: Story about founders' friendship turning into business
- Contact: Address, phone, email, opening hours, social links

## What's Been Implemented (Jan 2026)
- ✅ Responsive navigation with mobile menu
- ✅ Hero section with asymmetric Tetris-style layout
- ✅ Food menu with 17 items across 4 categories (Starters, Main Courses, Seafood Specials, Sides)
- ✅ Drinks menu with 5 categories (Rum Cocktails, Mocktails, Beer & Cider, Wine, Soft Drinks)
- ✅ About section with founders' story and values
- ✅ Contact section with all details and Google Maps link
- ✅ Footer with quick links and hours
- ✅ Scandi-Tropico design theme (tropical colors, clean European layout)
- ✅ Smooth scroll navigation
- ✅ Framer Motion animations

## Design System
- Colors: Warm Sand (#FDFCF8), Pink (#FF66A3), Green (#32CD32), Orange (#FFA500), Teal (#008080)
- Typography: Syne (headings), DM Sans (body), Space Grotesk (menu items)
- Style: Tropical Caribbean meets Scandinavian minimalism
